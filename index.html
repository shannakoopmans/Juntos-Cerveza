Better together


We believe that having a drink is more fun when you do it together. It doesn't matter if that person is someone you have known for a long time or if you just met. With this beer you will always get to know something new about the person you're sitting next to.

Nienke Licumahua

Hi there, my name is Nienke. My reasoning behind my faith in this brand is because I have a lot of friendships and connections already, but have trouble keeping up with them. I think this brand creates an experience for new connections, but also keeping up with old ones. I am very confident this brand can be a good addition to a new friendship forming or a reunion with an old friend. I hope everyone enjoys our cerveza!

Anique Duarte

Hi my name is Anique. My reason for developing this brand is simple, I love meeting new people. Sometimes it can be a bit tough for others to get along with my extroverted personality. I wanted to make it easier for people to connect with each other, and what way is better then enjoying a beer together. I hope everyone will enjoy our beer and make precious memories together.

Shanna Koopmans

Hello, I'm Shanna. The reason why I believe in this brand that we have created is because I have also had trouble connecting with others students, especially since I'm not from Breda and I think our brand helps with meeting new people or getting closer with others. That's why, when we started this brand, I really wanted this to be included in our beer. Hopefully you'll be able to grow friendships and enjoy our beer!
